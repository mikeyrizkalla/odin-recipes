Fruit Bowl

Because every morning needs to start off with a bang! And these Granola & Yogurt Bowls do just that. So yummy, so delicious, and they each only take about 5 minutes to make!

Description

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Banana
  • Raspberry
  • Blueberry
  • Chia Seeds

Steps

  1. Slice up the fruits
  2. Fill the bowl with yogurt
  3. Top with desired fruits
  4. Garnish with chia seeds
